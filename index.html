Interesting Facts about Flying-Cicadas

  • Flying-Cicdas are a group of sound-producing insects (order Homoptera). They have two pairs of membranous wings, prominent compound eyes, and three simple eyes (ocelli).
  • Cicadas are medium to large in size, ranging from 2 to 5 cm (0.8 to 2 inches).
  • Male cicadas produce loud noises by vibrating membranes (tymbals) near the base of the abdomen.
